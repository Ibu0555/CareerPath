About SkillSphere

AI-Powered Career Guidance Platform

Transforming career confusion into clarity with personalized skill mapping, gap analysis, and career roadmaps.

Purpose

SkillSphere is designed to bridge the gap between current skills and career aspirations through intelligent analysis and actionable insights. Our AI-powered platform helps job seekers and professionals navigate their career journey with confidence.

Target Audience

  • Job seekers looking for career direction
  • Professionals planning career advancement
  • Students exploring career options
  • Career counselors and recruiters

Skill Analysis

AI-powered resume parsing and skill extraction
